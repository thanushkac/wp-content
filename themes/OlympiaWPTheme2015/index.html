The Olympia steel buildings Advantage

Made of Steel. Built on Service ™

At Olympia Steel Buildings, we love to design and build. The project size and scope of your building doesn't matter. We offer complete, cost-effective metal and steel building solutions for a wide array of uses and applications.

Our portfolio includes garages, warehouses, industrial complexes, livestock shelters, airplane hangers and churches. While our projects vary, our goal is always the same—to help you realize your vision and get the best return on your investment. Whether you need it for commercial, agricultural, industrial or residential use, we provide pre-engineered steel buildings at competitive prices.

We don't just promise great service—we deliver it.

When you choose to work with Olympia Steel Buildings, you'll work with a highly experienced steel building company known for superior workmanship and a friendly, knowledgeable team. You can be assured your project will be built to your complete satisfaction.

All of our steel and metal buildings come with a 35-year rust-through perforation warranty on AZ55 Galvalume® roof panels and are made with 100% American steel. Since we provide flexible and green building design [Link to http://www.usgbc.org/organizations/universal-steel-buildings-corp], you can incorporate any custom feature as well as contemporary aesthetics [Link to Features]. The end result is a beautifully designed, sustainable and virtually maintenance-free steel building that will last you a lifetime.
